Our Story

Our dough tells a story of tradition, heritage, and the vibrant flavors of the Levant. With half of our roots in Lebanon and the other in Palestine, we bring together the best of both worlds.

From Palestine, we use the finest olive oil and the perfect blend of za'atar, honoring flavors passed down through generations. From Lebanon, we embrace the authentic taste of manakish.
