Manakish Menu

Freshly baked Manakish, prepared with authentic flavors and the finest ingredients. Each piece is crafted with care, following traditional Levantine recipes passed down through generations.

Our signature offerings include:

  • Manakish Za'atar: A traditional blend of thyme, sesame, and sumac on soft dough
  • Manakish Cheese: Topped with a generous layer of melted cheese for a rich, savory taste
  • Manakish Beef: Premium ground beef seasoned with our special blend of Levantine spices
